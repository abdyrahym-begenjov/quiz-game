===============================================================================================
                   			 🤔  QUIZ — GAME RULES  📯
===============================================================================================

This is a multilingual quiz game (supporting English and Russian) that saves your user profile, 
tracks high scores, and challenges you across two distinct gameplay modes.


📜 General Rules
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Setup: On your first run, you will be prompted to select your language and enter your name. Your name 
and progression are saved across sessions.
* Answering Questions: For every question, you must type a valid letter corresponding to the choices 
provided (A, B, C, D for English or А, Б, В, Г for Russian). Invalid responses will not be accepted, 
and you will be forced to try again.
* Question Pool: Once a question has been asked, it is removed from the active question list for that 
session so you will not see duplicates.


🎮 Breakdown of Game Modes
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1) Timer Mode

In this mode, you race against a ticking clock to answer a fixed set of questions.

Difficulty Parameters:
* Easy: 15 questions, starting with 45 seconds.
* Normal: 25 questions, starting with 60 seconds.
* Hard: 40 questions, starting with 90 seconds.
* Dangerous: 40 questions, starting with 90 seconds, featuring a "surprise" penalty mechanism.

Rules & Time Mechanics:
* Answering a question correctly awards you an extra 5 seconds.
* Answering incorrectly penalizes your time by the number of seconds it took you to think about that 
specific answer.
* Dangerous Surprise: If playing on "Dangerous" difficulty and you guess wrong, there is a hidden "bomb" 
among the incorrect answers. If your wrong answer triggers the bomb, you immediately lose an additional 
10 seconds.
* Sudden Death: If your correct and incorrect counts are completely tied at the end of the question pool, 
the game gives you one final tie-breaker question to determine your ultimate victory or loss.
* Winning/Scoring Conditions: If your time drops to 00:00 or below, it's an immediate Game Over.
* If you finish all questions, your rank is determined by accuracy: Perfect scores give you "You Win!!!" 
(3 stars), $\ge$ 70% accuracy gives "Good Work!!!" (2 stars), and $>50\%$ accuracy gives "Weak, but still 
you're great!!!" (1 star). Anything below or equal to 50% triggers a Game Over.
* Final points are calculated using your regular correct answers and remaining time, divided by your 
penalizing mistakes to ensure a balanced leaderboard score.



2) Infinity Mode

This mode tests your long-term survival rather than speed. There is no timer, allowing you to take as 
much time as you need per question.

Rules & Mechanics:
* You begin the game with 3 Heart (❤️) lives.
* Every correct answer awards you 1 point.
* Every incorrect answer deducts 1 Heart life.
* End Game: The game ends immediately when you lose all 3 hearts (Game Over) or if you successfully clear 
out the entire question database pool, crowning you the "ABSOLUTE CHAMPION!!!". Your accumulated points 
are saved straight to the leaderboard.


🕹️ Other Modes
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The main menu features four structural modes outside of direct 
gameplay to help you manage your profile, view information, and exit safely.

1) Rules Mode

This mode dynamically reads and displays the game rules directly from external text files based on your 
currently selected language. It will load and print the contents of `ru_rules.txt` if your language is 
set to Russian, or `en_rules.txt` if your language is set to English. It then waits for you to press Enter 
before returning to the main menu.

2) Highscores Mode

This mode fetches data from your local save file (`base.json`) and dynamically renders a formatted scoreboard.
It showcases your name alongside your cumulative acquired points, cleanly split between both "Timer" and 
"Infinity" modes.

3) Settings Mode

Allows you to update your profile variables on the fly. You can change your active username or swap 
the game's language between English and Russian. Switching the language automatically updates the active 
text translation maps and re-indexes the question file pool (`q1.json` for English, `q2.json` for Russian).

4) Exit Mode

Safely closes out the quiz game. It triggers an exit confirmation prompt ("Yes" or "No") to ensure you don't 
accidentally close your application and lose your current session navigation.
